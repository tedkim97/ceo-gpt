Welcome to CeoGPT

CeoGPT is our flagship, 3 quintillion parameter LLM meant to replace the CEOs. Our model has been trained on interviews, books, and tweets from the greatest thinkers and leaders such as: Adam Neumann, Charlie Javice, Elizabeth Holmes, Jack Welch, Jeffrey Skilling, Martin Shkreli, Sam Bankman-Fried and many more.

Elon Musk GPT

As the CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, the founder of several companies, and one of the top Diablo-4 players, Elon Musk's datapoints have an outsized effect on the CEO training data. As a result, we've decided to separate him from the traditional CeoGPT and train a new model exclusively on his tweets.

Traditional Ceo GPT

For some reason, today's CEOs still refer to the knowledge of CEO's from traditional, provably inefficient leadership models. So this model is trained on that.
